My work process

1. Initial Meeting

Contact me in any way you prefer (phone, e-mail, message). To get basic info about your existing or future interior (object under construction, etc.) I usually use a short questionnaire. The introductory interview and your answers will help me get the best possible information about your needs, ideas, and demands for your future new interior before our first personal meeting.

2. Space Measurement

This is followed by a personal meeting associated with the inspection, photo documentation and measurement of all rooms that will be affected by the design / implementation of the interior. In the case of a non-existent interior, the project documentation is used. This stage is the absolute basic and starting point from which all other works and their scope are derived. This also creates and clarifies the basis for determining the price for the services of an interior designer. The subsequent phase results in the creation of the drawing current disposition.

3. Available Solutions

In this phase, one (max. Two) of the best and most suitable interior layout solutions is implemented in terms of its area options, ergonomics, expected purpose, statics, existing or planned changes to the installation (connections, networks, etc.), etc. At the same time, the whole part of this process is based on the client's assignment with the intention of arriving at the most ideal layout solution, which will take into account all his needs, wishes and ideas.

4. Interior Study Development

This phase is one of the most interesting for many. She is very joyful, playful and creative. We are looking for and creating the style, mood and atmosphere of your future interior. In the form of a moodboard (mood board in the form of a collage) and sketches, I will introduce you to the key details of the interior: color, furniture shapes, materials, lamps, fixtures, etc.

5. Material Selection

Following the previous step, if the client is interested in the implementation project, the phase of selection of specific materials and furnishing items from which the price offer of the implementation is derived follows.

6. Realistic 3D Drawing

This phase is one of the most interesting for many. She is very joyful, playful and creative. We are looking for and creating the style, mood and atmosphere of your future interior. In the form of a moodboard (mood board in the form of a collage) and sketches, I will introduce you to the key details of the interior: color, furniture shapes, materials, lamps, fixtures, etc.

7. Basic Technical Background

The basic technical documentation of the project contains all the necessary documents for individual professions. Based on the finished implementation project, I will calculate an estimate of the work for you, as well as the cost of production and assembly of custom-made furniture and other furnishing elements.

8. Implementation itself & Supervision

During this period, site visits are carried out, and the compliance of the construction work performed is checked according to the interior study. The last adjustments are made and the details are fine-tuned.

9. Finished Object

After the approval of the completed project, you become proud the owner of the new interior.
