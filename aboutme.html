My Story

Hello, I am
Tamara Tarttot and

I love to laugh and talk to people, I could use my gift of speech and love for interacting with people in different domains I had a chance to try, however, there was still something missing. Even though I was surrounded by people with whom I could talk to and laugh with, I had a need to do something ''higher'' where I can implement my creativity. It was unless further in my life when I finally did the big step and decided to go for what I was attracted to for a long time and that was Interior Design.

''HOME is a place where you have to feel safe, where you like to return, Where you can be yourself.''

How did I get into Interior Design?

Before I was accepted to the University of Media and Marketing Communication, I applied to college with a focus on Urbanism and Architecture, to be honest, I do not even know how this direction came to my mind but unfortunately, I was not accepted and I viewed it as a sign. Another sign came when after the first semester of Media and Marketing Communication they canceled my field of study.

After this I began to wonder where to work as sitting at home was not an option, so I started to work in our family business and started selling beautiful jewelry whose designers and manufacturers were my parents. After some time my mother and I opened our own jewelry store that had to undergo reconstruction and for this, we hired an interior designer. I was actively involved in the design. We had a fairly clear idea, but you still have some doubts as to whether it will turn out well in the end.

At that time, during one of the consultations with the interior designer, she told me, something I call an impulse that sometimes we all need, that I could focus on interior design because I think of details that are very important from a functional point of view. She must have seen the enthusiasm and, above all, the joy I experienced during each consultation. Yes, that's exactly the feeling when you feel like a "fish in water".

I admit that I no longer remember exactly the moment when I decided to move my findings regarding my professional orientation further, but the result was the completion of an accredited study of interior design. Interior design has thus become my dream profession, which I have been actively, enthusiastically but also in love with for the third year.

So I've been close to design and art all my life, but I seem to get to interior design as if by accident. Or is it true that there are no coincidences? Be that as it may, I thank my "coincidence" and I am very grateful for it ...

What is inspiring me?

Each of us needs inspiration. It is a kind of motivation that strikes you at some point and drives you forward. I thought for a moment about what inspired me, and I realized that it was nothing but the people themselves. People who trust what I can do for them, who long for a beautiful cozy home where they can grow and be themselves. That is why I perceive interior design as one of the ways that allows people to live a better and more beautiful life.

Creation of home

Creating a home for ourselves or our family is one of the most beautiful, but often times the most challenging periods in life. Why? Today we have a myriad of options for furnishing our houses and apartments. The Internet is full of various inspirations and constantly changing trends, companies and stores with an interior assortment full of gadgets. Overpressure of possibilities often results in us getting lost in it and reaching a stage where we no longer know what we want, what we like. In addition, few of us have time in our responsibilities and can afford to devote hours to decision-making and implementation.

How can I help you?

My job is to help you get out of this vicious circle. To help you organize your thoughts, name your real needs and demands for your housing, all with a budget. The result is the design of YOUR HOME in which you will be able to grow, to develop, to which you will be happy to return.

I will be more than happy to take part in creating your dream HOME
